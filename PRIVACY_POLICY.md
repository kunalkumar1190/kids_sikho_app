# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 17, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Anganwadi Kids**. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, educational experience for children. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the Anganwadi Kids application.

## 2. Information Collection and Use
We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) from children or parents. The app is designed to be used safely without requiring user registration, email addresses, or phone numbers.

- **Offline Usage:** The core features of our app work offline and do not transmit data to external servers.
- **Local Storage:** Any progress or settings (such as game scores or preferences) are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us.

## 3. Third-Party Services
Our application does not include third-party analytics or advertising frameworks that track user behavior across other apps and websites. 

## 4. Children's Privacy
We strictly adhere to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar global standards. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no risk of children's data being shared or misused.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and provide notice within the application.
